# Refund & Cancellation Policy

**Effective date:** 11 July 2026
**Applies to:** Vachanam subscription plans and voice-minute charges billed to clinics.
**Operated by:** Vinay Rongala (sole proprietor), Hyderabad, Telangana, India · hello@vachanam.in

Vachanam is a B2B service for clinics. Patients are never charged by Vachanam —
this policy is for clinic owners who pay for a Vachanam plan.

## 1. Free trial — no charge, no card

Every clinic starts with a 14-day free trial (300 voice minutes included). No
payment details are collected for the trial. If you do not pay when the trial
ends, service simply pauses — nothing is charged, nothing is owed.

## 2. Cancelling your subscription

- You can cancel any time by emailing **hello@vachanam.in** from your registered
  owner email, or from your dashboard.
- Cancellation takes effect at the end of the month you have already paid for.
  Your AI receptionist keeps working until then.
- No cancellation fee. No lock-in. No minimum contract.
- After cancellation your data is exported for you on request and then deleted
  per our [Privacy Policy](/privacy) retention schedule.

## 3. Refunds

- **Duplicate or erroneous payment:** refunded in full. Email us with the
  payment reference; we refund within 7 working days to the original payment
  method via Razorpay.
- **Charged after cancellation:** any amount charged for a period after your
  effective cancellation date is refunded in full.
- **Service failure:** if a verified fault on our side kept your AI
  receptionist down for more than 24 continuous hours in a billed month, we
  credit a pro-rata amount to your next invoice (or refund it if you are
  cancelling).
- **Change of mind mid-month:** monthly subscription fees for the current,
  already-started billing month are non-refundable. Included minutes do not
  carry over or convert to cash.
- **Overage charges:** per-minute overage reflects calls actually answered for
  your clinic and is non-refundable, except when caused by a verified fault on
  our side (e.g. calls double-billed), which is refunded in full.

## 4. How refunds are processed

Refunds go back to the original payment method through Razorpay. Timelines
after we approve a refund: UPI/wallet 2–4 working days; cards/netbanking 5–7
working days (bank dependent). GST on a refunded amount is reversed by credit
note.
