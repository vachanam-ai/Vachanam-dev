title: Free trial and billing
audience: both
category: billing
tags: trial, billing, payment, razorpay, invoice
---
Every clinic starts with a 14-day free trial — no card needed, about 100 calls
included. Near the end of the trial you get a secure payment link to activate
your plan, and you can pay by UPI, card, or netbanking through Razorpay. If the
trial ends unpaid, your line pauses but nothing is deleted, and paying later
reactivates it instantly. You can change your plan any time from Settings; a
change takes effect from the next billing cycle so you never lose minutes you
already paid for.
