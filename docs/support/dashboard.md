The clinic owner dashboard shows how Vachanam is helping your clinic: how many
calls came in, how many turned into bookings, your busiest hours, and how many
free minutes remain this month. Open it from the **Dashboard** tab after signing
in. Every call and its outcome is listed, so you can see exactly what happened on
any call. The dashboard never shows revenue estimates — it focuses on calls,
bookings, and usage.
