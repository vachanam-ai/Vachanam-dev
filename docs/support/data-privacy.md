title: Data privacy and patient information
audience: both
category: technical
tags: privacy, dpdp, data, security, patient
---
Vachanam keeps each clinic's data fully separated — no clinic can ever see
another clinic's patients or calls. We store only what is needed to book
appointments (name, phone, token), never medical records, diagnoses, or test
results, in line with India's DPDP Act 2023. Notifications never contain health
details. You can read the full policy at vachanam.in/privacy. When you contact
support, please describe the issue without pasting patient names, phone numbers,
or health details.
