---
title: Connecting your phone number
audience: clinic
category: onboarding
tags: did, number, phone, forwarding
---
Connecting your phone number to Vachanam takes a few minutes. After a number is
provisioned for your clinic, add it in Settings under Telephony, then set your
clinic line to forward calls to it. Once forwarding is active, Vachanam answers
every call in your chosen language and books appointments automatically. If
calls are not being answered, check that call forwarding is switched on at your
telecom provider.
