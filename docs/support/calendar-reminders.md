title: Google Calendar, reminders and follow-ups
audience: clinic
category: onboarding
tags: calendar, google, reminder, follow-up, notification
---
Every confirmed booking is written to your Google Calendar automatically, so
your doctors see appointments in the calendar they already use. Connect it in
**Settings** by authorising Google Calendar for your clinic. Patients and doctors
are notified about bookings, and Vachanam can place a reminder call before an
appointment so fewer patients miss their slot. On the Clinic and Multi plans,
Vachanam can also make follow-up calls after a visit. Calendar events only carry
the patient's first name, last four digits of their phone, and the token — never
any medical details.
