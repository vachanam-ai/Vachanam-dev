Vachanam has three plans, all prices excluding 18% GST. Starter is ₹5,999 per
month and includes 1 number and about 250 calls (700 minutes) with one doctor.
Clinic is ₹9,999 per month with about 540 calls (1,500 minutes), up to five
doctors, and Telugu, Hindi and English. Multi is ₹17,999 per month with about
1,080 calls (3,000 minutes), unlimited doctors, and all eight languages. Extra
usage is billed at ₹5 per minute on every plan. You can start with a 14-day
free trial (no card, about 100 calls) before you pay.
