---
title: A call was not answered or booking failed
audience: both
category: technical
tags: call, failed, not working, booking
---
If a call was not answered, first check that your clinic line is forwarding to
your Vachanam number and that your plan still has minutes left. If a booking did
not complete, the caller may have hung up before confirming — Vachanam never
holds a token without a confirmed booking, so nothing is double-booked. You can
see every call and its outcome on your dashboard. If the problem continues,
describe what happened here and our team will look into it.
