audience: clinic
category: onboarding
tags: doctor, staff, add, team
---
To add a doctor, open Settings and go to the Doctors section, then add the
doctor's name and working hours. Vachanam routes each patient to the right
doctor based on the issue and the time they want. The number of doctors you can
add depends on your plan: one on Starter, up to five on Clinic, and unlimited on
Multi. Receptionists use the mobile app to mark attendance and manage the queue.
