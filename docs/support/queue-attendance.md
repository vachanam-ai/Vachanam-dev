---
title: Managing the queue and marking attendance
audience: clinic
category: onboarding
tags: queue, token, attendance, walk-in, receptionist, no-show
---
Every booking gets a token number, and no two patients ever get the same token
for the same doctor and time. Your receptionist opens the **Queue** page on the
mobile app to see the day's tokens in order, mark a patient as attended when they
arrive, or mark a no-show if they don't. You can also add a **Walk-in** patient
directly from the app, which assigns the next token safely. The waiting-room TV
board (the /tv screen) shows current token numbers to patients without revealing
any names or phone numbers.
