---
title: Cancellations, rescheduling and doctor leave
audience: both
category: technical
tags: cancel, reschedule, change, doctor leave, rebook
---
A patient can call the clinic number again to cancel or change an appointment,
and Vachanam updates the token and calendar automatically. If a doctor goes on
leave, mark it under **Doctor leave** in the app — Vachanam then calls the
affected patients to rebook them with another suitable time or doctor, so nobody
is left stranded. A token is only ever held for a confirmed booking, so a cancelled
or abandoned call never blocks a slot for someone else.
