title: What is Vachanam?
audience: both
category: onboarding
tags: about, what, receptionist, ai
---
Vachanam is an AI voice receptionist for Indian clinics. When a patient calls
your clinic number, Vachanam answers in your chosen language, understands the
health issue, matches the right doctor, checks availability, and books an
appointment with a token — usually within a few minutes. Your receptionist
never misses a call, even when the clinic is busy or closed. Vachanam does not
give medical advice; it only books appointments and passes on your clinic's own
emergency contact when a caller asks for a human.
