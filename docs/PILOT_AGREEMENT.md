## What the clinic gets (free for 14 days)

- Vachanam AI receptionist on a dedicated phone number: answers patient
  calls in the clinic's language, books appointments with token numbers,
  creates calendar entries, and notifies the doctor — 24×7.
- Up to **300 minutes** of AI call time (≈100 calls). Service pauses
  automatically if the cap is reached early.
- Personal onboarding and support by the founder throughout the pilot.

| 3 | Missed-call revenue recovered (bookings × avg consultation ₹____) | ≥ ₹ ____ |

The Vachanam dashboard shows these numbers live during the pilot.

## What happens on day 14

- **If the success criteria are met**, the clinic continues on the
  **______ plan at the launch-offer price of ₹______/month** (offer price
  applies for the first 3 paid months, then standard price). The first
  payment is due on day 14; service continues without interruption.
- If the clinic chooses not to continue, it says so in writing (WhatsApp/
  email is fine) **on or before day 14**. Service stops, nothing is owed,
  and clinic data is deleted on request.
- If the criteria are **not** met, the clinic owes nothing and both sides
  part as friends.

## Clinic's part during the pilot

- Forward / publish the Vachanam number for patient calls.
- Keep doctor timings in the dashboard accurate.
- Staff marks attendance in the app so the numbers above are real.
- Give honest feedback — that is the founding clinic's real contribution.

## Data & conduct

- Patient data belongs to the clinic; Vachanam processes it only to run
  the service (DPDP Act 2023). No patient data is shared with anyone.
- The AI never gives medical advice; emergencies surface the clinic's own
  emergency contact.
- No call audio is recorded.
